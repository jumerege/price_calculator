🚀 The Space Logistics Challenge

🛸

Commercial Space Stations

Limited experimental slots. Expensive recurring costs. No guaranteed sample retrieval. Long waiting times.

  • Sharing payload space
  • Extended mission durations
  • High ongoing fees
✈️

Parabolic Flights

Only 20-30 seconds of microgravity per parabola. Low-quality data. Severe motion artifacts. Not true space conditions.

  • Brief exposure time
  • Vibration & motion
  • No orbital environment
🧪

Drop Towers

Maximum 10 seconds of free fall. Limited to facilities in specific locations. Cannot replicate orbital conditions.

  • Ultra-short duration
  • Gravity constraints
  • Fixed infrastructure
